Turkey Burgers

A delicious turkey burger

Ingridients

  • One and a half pounds ground turkey
  • One large egg
  • One (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix
  • One and a half tablespoons soy sauce
  • Half teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Half teaspoon garlic powder
  • Hamburger buns

Steps

  1. Mix turkey, egg, onion soup mix, soy sauce, pepper, and garlic powder in a large bowl until well combined
  2. Refrigerate mixture for about 10 minutes, then form into 6 equal-sized patties
  3. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate
  4. Cook turkey burgers on the preheated grill, turning once, until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear
  5. Serve on buns
